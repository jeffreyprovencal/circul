The problem

460 million tonnes per year.
9% recycled.

The infrastructure to collect and process recyclable waste exists. The problem is coordination — and data.

Problem 01

No visibility

Processors can't see what's available, where, or when. Collectors don't know where to take material. Everyone operates in the dark.

Problem 02

No traceability

Paper receipts and phone logs don't create audit trails. Brands and certifiers have no way to verify where recycled content actually came from.

Problem 03

No income data

Collectors — the backbone of the system — have no proof of earnings, no transaction history, no path to financial inclusion.

How it works

From street to factory,
one connected chain.

Each tier uses a tool designed for their context — from USSD on a basic phone to a full analytics dashboard.

COLLECTOR

Log collections by weight and material

Collectors record pickups via USSD or mobile app. Each collection is timestamped, geotagged, and linked to their account — building a verifiable earnings history.

📱 USSD & mobile app · No smartphone required
AGGREGATOR

Receive, sort, and batch material

Aggregators confirm handoffs from collectors, sort by material type, and create batches ready for processor pickup — all tracked in real time.

🗂 Web dashboard · Batch management
PROCESSOR

Source, verify, and process batches

Processors see available supply in real time, request pickups, and get full traceability on every batch they receive — with certified provenance attached.

📊 Analytics dashboard · Supply sourcing
CONVERTER

Buy certified recycled content

Converters access processors with verified output data, attach ESG-ready documentation to every purchase, and demonstrate circular sourcing to their clients.

📋 Compliance reports · ESG documentation
