Why Circul

Why Circul?

📱

Works on any phone

No smartphone required. Collectors log via mobile web — no app download, no data plan needed. USSD access coming soon.

💰

Price transparency before you sell

Know what aggregators are paying before you show up. Live buy prices for PET, HDPE, PP, and LDPE — always visible, always honest.

🔗

Full chain-of-custody

Every kilogram is tagged from collection point to factory floor. Brands and regulators can verify the entire journey with one link.

⭐

Mutual ratings system

Collectors rate aggregators. Aggregators rate collectors. A trust layer that rewards reliability and surfaces the best partners.

📋

Compliance reports, one click

EPR and audit reports generated automatically from your transaction data. Export to PDF or structured data — ready when regulators ask.

🪪

Collector Passport

Every collector builds a digital record of what they've collected and earned — a portable identity that grows with their work.
